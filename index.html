The Heritage of Harsha

Shimla is well known for its matchless hospitality and cultural diversity. Finding accommodation in Shimla has never been a problem for the travelers. All Shimla hotels strive hard to provide the best hotel services to their guests. We at Hotel Harsha provide you luxury accommodation at the City Center of Shimla and that too at economic prices. Travelers who come from different parts of the world start their journey into Himalayas from here. Our hotel is a renowned budget hotel in the category of budget hotels in Shimla.

We are one of most experienced and renowned hotels in Shimla, one of the few Hotels offering Drive in Facility. We provide quick online hotel booking to all our guests from different parts of the world. We also provide pick up to all our guests so that they do not face any hustles when they arrive at Shimla. We provide exciting discounts from time to time to our guests. The Hotel is approved by the Department of Tourism & Civil Aviation, Government of Himachal Pradesh.

Experience the fine art of hospitality at Hotel Harsha, Shimla. We go the extra mile to make sure you have a great time in Shimla. We provide exquisite facilities as compared to other hotels in shimla. We have wide varieties of rooms including Standard room, Deluxe room, Superior room, Family room & Suite.
